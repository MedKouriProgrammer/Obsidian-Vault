# Breakfast

- 70g Oats
- 1banana
- 30g Peanut Butter

**Ingredients**

- [ ]  2 pounds mussels cleaned and debearded
- [ ]  3 tablespoons ghee
- [ ]  5 garlic cloves minced
- [ ]  1 cup fresh mango puree
- [ ]  ½ cup dry white wine
- [ ]  ¼ teaspoon turmeric
- [ ] ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- [ ]  ¼ teaspoon salt
- [ ]  Cilantro chopped for garnish

**Method**

1. Wash the mussels in a colander and debeard them (remove any brown strands). Discard any mussels that are cracked or remain open even after tapping them on a hard surface.
2. Melt ghee in a large dutch oven over medium heat. Stir in the garlic and after 30 seconds, add the mango puree, white wine and spices.
3. Bring the liquid to a boil, add the mussels and cover the pot for 3 minutes.
4. Remove the lid, give the mussels a stir, then cover for another 3 minutes, or until the mussel shells open. Discard any mussels that do not open.
5. Place the mussels in a shallow serving bowl and ladle the liquid on top. Garnish with chopped cilantro.
