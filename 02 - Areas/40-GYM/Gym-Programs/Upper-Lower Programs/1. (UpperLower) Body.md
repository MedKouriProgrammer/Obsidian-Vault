### <font color="#646a73">Programs : (U-L-Body.)</font>

1. **<font color="#938953">Definition:</font>** <font color="#a5a5a5">This program trains either the upper or lower body in a day, <u>Spread over four days a week.</u></font>
2. <font color="#00b050">The benefits:</font> <font color="#a5a5a5">Because this programs training the muscles 4 days a week, <u>This means working out faster, Training the muscles twice a week in less time.</u> </font>
3. <font color="#ff0000">Disadvantages:</font><font color="#a5a5a5"> The drawbacks of these programs is the training all upperbody or lowerbody in one day, <u>That means you’ll have little energy left for the final exercises.</u></font>
