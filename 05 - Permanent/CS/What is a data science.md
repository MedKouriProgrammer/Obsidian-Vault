Data science is the process of extracting knowledge and insights from data using a variety of tools, techniques, and algorithms. Data science combines the fields of statistics, mathematics, computer science, and domain knowledge to analyze and interpret data. It is used to solve complex problems in areas such as healthcare, finance, marketing, and engineering.
