# Who is a Sigma person? 🐺
A sigma male is a type of man who is independent and strong-willed. He does not conform to the traditional gender roles and expectations of society and is more likely to challenge the status quo. He is often seen as a leader and is highly capable and successful in his endeavors. He is not afraid to take risks and is comfortable with his own identity. He is often seen as a loner, but he is also capable of forming strong relationships with those he trusts.
