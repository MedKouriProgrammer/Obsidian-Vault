Self-awareness is the ability to reflect on one's own thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. It involves recognizing one’s strengths and weaknesses, understanding how one's actions affect others, and being able to accurately assess one's emotions and motivations. Self-awareness is an important part of emotional intelligence and can help people make better decisions, build stronger relationships, and lead more fulfilling lives.


A self-conscious person is someone who is overly aware of their own behavior, appearance, or actions. They may be overly concerned with how others perceive them, and feel anxious or embarrassed in social situations. They may also be overly critical of themselves and their abilities.
