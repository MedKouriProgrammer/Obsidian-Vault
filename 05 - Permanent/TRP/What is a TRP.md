# What is a TRP Σ↜🐺💊↝Σ
The Red Pill (TRP) ideology is a set of beliefs and attitudes that are popular among certain groups of men on the internet. It is based on the idea that men are oppressed by society, and that they should reject traditional gender roles and strive to become more powerful and successful. It also promotes the idea that men should be more assertive and confident, and that they should take control of their lives and relationships. The term "Red Pill" comes from the 1999 movie The Matrix, where the protagonist is offered a red pill that will reveal the truth about the world.
