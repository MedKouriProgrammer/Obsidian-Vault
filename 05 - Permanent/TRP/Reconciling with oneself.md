Reconciling with oneself is the process of accepting and understanding one’s own thoughts, feelings, and behaviors. It involves taking responsibility for one’s actions and developing an understanding of how those actions have impacted oneself and others. It also involves being honest with oneself about one’s strengths and weaknesses, and understanding how to use those strengths and weaknesses to one’s advantage. Reconciling with oneself can be a difficult and lengthy process, but it is an important step in personal growth and development.
