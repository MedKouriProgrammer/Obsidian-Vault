# What Is Monk Mode?
Monk mode is a period of enhanced focus, [[Self discipline|discipline]], and productivity where you commit yourself to completing a goal. In monk mode, you take it upon yourself to adopt the isolation and [[Self discipline|self-discipline]] practices of monks. In modern times, being in a monk-like state has been made famous for self-improvement. It is described as an attempt to cope with denying ourselves of all situations and activities by gaining control of our emotional state while pursuing our goals for growth.
