An independent person is someone who is self-reliant, capable of making decisions and taking actions without relying on others. They are confident and independent-minded, and able to think for themselves. They are not easily influenced by external forces and can stand up for what they believe in. They are also able to think critically and objectively, and make decisions based on facts and evidence rather than emotion.
