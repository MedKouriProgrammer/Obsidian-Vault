Self control is the ability to regulate one's emotions, thoughts, and behaviors in order to achieve goals. It is a skill that can be developed and improved through practice and dedication. Self control is important in many areas of life, including relationships, work, and school. It can help one stay focused, organized, and productive, as well as make better decisions. Self control can also help one resist temptation and make healthier choices.
